OG Filename: CIRCUITS STEMS Finished, unreleased song intended to be included in the scrapped album Turbo. Circuits was performed live several times around 2017-2018, but was scrapped in late 2018 when focus shifted to Ugly is Beautiful, and the song gained notoriety amongst Oliver's fans who wanted to see it released.
