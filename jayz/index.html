Dr. Dre - Under Pressure [V1](feat. Jay-Z) (prod. Dr. Dre)
"Under Pressure" was the first major "Detox" leak, surfacing online in July 2010. It was first announced in April of the same year, planned for a single release that was delayed in favor of Eminem's "Not Afraid". Dre released a statement on the leak afterward, confirming its authenticity and that it was unfinished. The instrumental appeared in an HP commercial shortly after the leak, but never released as a single. Generally mixed to negative reactions to the song online after its leak may be responsible for it being scrapped. The song marked a reunion between Scott Storch and Dre, who hadn't been on good terms or worked together since the early-2000s. Interpolates "I Smoke, I Drank" by Body Head Bangerz.
Dr. Dre - Under Pressure [V3](feat. Jay-Z & Diablo Vazquez) (prod. Dr. Dre)
Later version of "Under Pressure" featuring little-known artist Diablo Vazquez filling in the previously open sections of the song with a hook, often mistaken for Sly Pyper. The legitimacy and origin of this version is a bit unclear, it surfaced sometime around September 2010, but given the artists' obscurity and the song having leaked already it's possible it's a remix of sorts being passed off as official. Only a very low-quality version has surfaced.

Future - I Been Drinking(prod. Detail)(Drunk In Love)
In 2014, Future claimed that he had recorded a demo of "Drunk in Love" with Detail. Detail convinced Future to record vocals, saying it would increase the chances of Beyoncé recording it. However, after several days, Detail informed Future that Beyoncé was working on the song.
